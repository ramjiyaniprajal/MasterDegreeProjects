FEEDBACK

LET US KNOW WHAT YOU THINK

Museum takes your views very seriously. Your feedback helps us improve our facilities and helps us understand what works and what doesn’t. Together we can continue to develop Museum to be a fantastic asset to the town.

You can leave feedback after your visit to the Museum. There are visitor feedback forms at reception, and also a Visitors' Book. If you didn’t pick up the form, you can download a PDF version from this website and either post, email, or drop it in to us.

You can also email any comments or queries you may have.

Use the links on the right to access the feedback form (these are PDF documents).
